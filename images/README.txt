HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PHOTO
==============================

STEP 1: Copy your photo to this folder (images/)

STEP 2: Rename your photo to one of these:
   - profile.jpg
   - profile.png
   - profile.jpeg

STEP 3: Open index.html and find this line (around line 35):
   <!-- <img src="images/profile.jpg" alt="Kannuru Srinadh"> -->

STEP 4: Remove the <!-- and --> to uncomment it:
   <img src="images/profile.jpg" alt="Kannuru Srinadh">

STEP 5: Remove or comment out the icon line:
   <!-- <i class="fas fa-user"></i> -->

RECOMMENDED PHOTO SPECIFICATIONS:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 800x1000 pixels (portrait orientation)
- File size: Less than 500KB for faster loading
- Background: Professional or plain background
- Quality: High resolution, well-lit photo

That's it! Your photo will appear on the website.
