HOW TO ADD YOUR CV/RESUME
==========================

STEP 1: Save your CV as PDF
   - Recommended filename: Kannuru_Srinadh_CV.pdf
   - Or use: Resume.pdf, CV.pdf

STEP 2: Place the PDF in this folder (documents/)

STEP 3: If you used a different filename, update index.html:
   
   Find this line (around line 20):
   <a href="documents/Kannuru_Srinadh_CV.pdf" download class="btn-download-cv">
   
   Change to your filename:
   <a href="documents/YOUR_FILENAME.pdf" download class="btn-download-cv">

STEP 4: Test the download button on your website!

CV RECOMMENDATIONS:
-------------------
✓ Format: PDF (most compatible)
✓ File size: Under 2MB
✓ Filename: No spaces (use underscores or hyphens)
✓ Content: Updated with latest achievements
✓ Design: Professional and clean

The gold "Download CV" button will appear in the hero section!
